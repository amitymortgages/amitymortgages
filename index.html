Commercial Mortgage for Residential Property: What Dubai Investors Need to Know

Many investors in Dubai explore whether they can use a commercial mortgage to acquire a residential property—often to benefit from flexible financing structures, business-friendly lending terms, or portfolio diversification strategies. Expert advisers like Amity Mortgages help investors understand the rules, risks, and advantages associated with this type of financing.

Can You Use a Commercial Mortgage for a Residential Property?

In Dubai, lenders evaluate the intended use, ownership structure, and investment purpose before approving a mortgage. While commercial mortgages are designed for business-use assets, some investors may secure them for residential units if the property serves a rental or revenue-generating function. Understanding these policies is essential before applying for financing.

Why Investors Consider This Approach

A commercial mortgage may offer benefits such as higher borrowing limits, flexible repayment terms, and more room for corporate structuring. However, it may also come with stricter documentation requirements, higher due-diligence checks, and regulatory compliance obligations.
